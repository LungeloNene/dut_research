Research Outputs & Accreditation

Annually DUT’s Research Outputs are collated and submitted to the DHET for the allocation of government subsidy to universities. This important activity that accrues a significant amount of funding for the university from the government.  Academics and researchers are expected to participate actively by publishing journal articles, books, chapters or papers published in conference proceedings.

The DHET subsidies research outputs in the following categories:

  • Journal articles (research articles) in accredited journals
  • Peer-reviewed books/chapters in books
  • Peer-reviewed published conference proceedings
  • Creative Research Outputs

Please see the DHET’s policies here for further details
